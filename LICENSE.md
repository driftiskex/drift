## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

---

❌ **Injection Fails Immediately After Attach**
Roblox may not have fully loaded when you clicked Attach.
**Solution:** Wait until you see your character move in-game before attaching. Increase `injection_delay` to `1000ms` in your config if the issue persists.

❌ **Windows SmartScreen Blocks the Installer**
SmartScreen flags unsigned executables by default — this is not a virus detection.
**Solution:** Click **More Info** on the SmartScreen dialog, then click **Run Anyway**. The build hash on the release page confirms the file is genuine.

---

❌ **Android `.apk` Shows "App Not Installed"**
A conflicting partial install or insufficient storage is blocking the `.apk`.
**Solution:** Go to `Settings > Apps` and uninstall any previous **Drift Executor** version, clear your package installer cache, then retry the install.

---

❌ **iOS `.ipa` Opens Then Crashes Immediately**
The signing certificate has expired or was revoked.
**Solution:** Re-sign the `.ipa` using your signer app and re-trust the certificate under `Settings > General > VPN & Device Management`.

---

❌ **Script Hub Shows "Failed to Load"**
Network error or stale local cache is preventing hub data from fetching.
**Solution:** Open `drift_config.json` at `C:/Users/YourName/AppData/Roaming/DriftExecutor/`, set `"script_hub_cache": false`, save, and relaunch to force a fresh fetch.

---

❌ **Console Shows "Luau Bytecode Mismatch"**
Roblox pushed a client update that changed the bytecode version before Drift patched.
**Solution:** Check the release page for a new version. Enable `auto_update` in your config to prevent this going forward.
